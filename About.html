In this internship I have to performing
simulation of industrial robotic arm in
welding cell using RoboDk software.

RoboDk provides more than 200 industrial
robotic arm 3d model for performing
various operation like welding, pick and
place, colour spray,etc. First I have to
make CAD model of workpiece by using CAD
software. I prefered solidworks and make
exhast pipe as a workpiece.

For this project I used two robotic arm

pallet fixture, earthing wire and end
effector which are placed in end of the
robotic arm. I used some tools like welding
gun, gripper,etc was provided by RoboDk
software.
